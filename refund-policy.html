Refund Policy

1. Introduction

EditBuddy strives to provide exceptional services to our clients. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for services purchased. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this Refund Policy.

2. Refund Eligibility

  • Refunds may be issued for projects that are canceled before work has commenced.
  • If the Client is dissatisfied with the service and can provide valid reasons, EditBuddy will assess refund requests on a case-by-case basis.
  • Deposits paid for services are non-refundable once the project has commenced.

3. Non-Refundable Services

  • Completed projects or deliverables that meet the agreed-upon specifications are non-refundable.
  • Payments for hosting, domain registration, or third-party software are non-refundable once these services have been activated or transferred to the Client.
  • Custom development work or hours already spent on the project are not eligible for a refund.

4. Refund Process

  • The Client must submit a written request for a refund, including the reason and supporting evidence, to info@editbuddy.co.za.
  • Refund requests will be reviewed within 7-10 business days, and the Client will be informed of the outcome.
  • If approved, refunds will be processed within 14 business days and issued via the original payment method.

5. Partial Refunds

  • For projects that have commenced but are not completed, partial refunds may be granted based on the amount of work already completed.
  • The percentage of the refund will be calculated based on the proportion of uncompleted work and any non-refundable expenses incurred by EditBuddy.

6. Amendments to this Policy

EditBuddy reserves the right to update or amend this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated to Clients, and continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact Us

For further questions or concerns regarding this Refund Policy, please contact us at info@editbuddy.co.za.
