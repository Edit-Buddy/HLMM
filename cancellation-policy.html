Cancellation Policy

1. Introduction

We understand that circumstances may arise that require the cancellation of a project or service. This Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions for canceling services provided by EditBuddy.

2. Cancellation Terms

  • Clients may cancel their project or service by providing written notice via email to projects@editbuddy.co.za.
  • Cancellation is only valid once confirmed by EditBuddy in writing.
  • Any work completed prior to the cancellation will be billed accordingly and must be settled before the cancellation is finalized.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds for canceled projects are governed by our Refund Policy. Clients are encouraged to review the Refund Policy for detailed information regarding eligibility and procedures.

4. Non-Cancellable Services

  • Services such as domain registrations, third-party hosting, and completed deliverables are non-cancellable and non-refundable once initiated or completed.
  • Custom work that has already been approved or delivered is not eligible for cancellation or refund.

5. Amendments to This Policy

EditBuddy reserves the right to update or amend this Cancellation Policy at any time. Clients will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

6. Contact Us

For further questions or concerns regarding this Cancellation Policy, please contact us at info@editbuddy.co.za.
